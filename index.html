PROSPECTUS SUMMARY This Prospectus relates to the Public Issue of Equity Shares by The Home Store (LTD), a company incorporated under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2011. The Home Store LTD is a pioneering company in the wholesale and retail sector and is looking to raise equity through the issuance of 490,000 non-preference participating shares with a face value of LSL 30 each at a premium of LSL 50 per share. The funds raised through this issue will be utilized primarily for the expansion of retail facilities, establishment of wholesale facilities, creation of strategic partnerships with market stakeholders and fi nancing daily business operations.

ABOUT THE COMPANY The Home Store LTD, incorporated in 2024, is a pioneering company in the retail and wholesale sector, specializing in the sale of household goods and food items. Headquartered in Maqhaka, Berea Lesotho, The Home Store LTD aims to rapidly set itself up as one of the most innovative players in the retail and wholesale industry. The company’s mission is to revolutionize retail and wholesale business models by investing in convenience; the aim is to create a collective movement amongst small scale retailers and producers.
